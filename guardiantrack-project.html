GuardianTrack

Smart child safety tracking system with AI prediction, geo-fencing, and emergency escalation

In Development

GuardianTrack is actively under development. Check back soon for the live demo!

The Problem

In a world where child safety concerns are rising, parents and guardians need more than simple GPS apps. They need an intelligent, tamper-resistant system that provides real-time tracking with predictive AI capabilities, geo-fencing zones, and emergency escalation to authorities — all while being impossible for children to disable.

Core Features

Unstoppable Tracking

Children cannot disable their location unless the parent explicitly allows. Ensures continuous monitoring for maximum safety.

Signal/Device Loss Alert

If GPS or internet signal is cut, an instant alert is sent to parents and local police with the last known location.

Safety Enhancements

Geo-Fencing Zones

Parents define safe zones (home, school, playground). Leaving or entering unexpectedly triggers instant alerts.

Battery & Signal Monitor

Alerts parents if device battery drops below 10% or enters a weak-signal area for proactive monitoring.

Emergency Features

SOS Panic Button

Child can press one button to alert parents & emergency responders with live GPS, and optionally activate microphone/video stream.

Emergency Escalation Mode

If the phone is turned off or forcibly disabled, the system sends last location + timestamp to parents and authorities immediately.

Smart Technology

AI Movement Prediction

If updates stop, AI predicts possible movement routes based on last direction & speed using machine learning algorithms.

Offline Logging

Stores location data offline when connectivity is lost and syncs automatically once back online.

Role Management & Privacy

Role-Based Access

Only verified guardians can control permissions. Multi-level authentication ensures secure access.

Tamper-Proof Control

Children cannot alter or disable tracking features, ensuring continuous protection and monitoring.
